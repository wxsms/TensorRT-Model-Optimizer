Handles both static NVFP4 quantizers (with pre-computed per-block amax) and dynamic quantizers (computing from weight tensor).

Parameters:

  • weight_quantizer – The weight quantizer (static or dynamic).

  • weight (Tensor) – The weight tensor (used for shape in static, values in dynamic).

  • weights_scaling_factor_2 (Tensor | None) – Optional pre-computed global scale.

  • keep_high_precision (bool) – Whether to keep scales in high precision.

Returns:
